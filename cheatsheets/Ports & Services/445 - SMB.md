# TCP 445 (SMB)

## Overview 

During Windows enumeration, port 445 is used for SMB, which provides file sharing and network resource access. It is one of the most commonly targeted services in internal networks due to frequent misconfigurations such as open shares, weak permissions, or exposed sensitive files. SMB enumeration often reveals valuable information such as users, passwords, shares and system structure.
